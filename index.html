About Environment

The environment includes all living and non-living things around us such as air, water, land, plants, and animals. It is essential for life on Earth. A clean environment provides fresh air, safe water, and healthy living conditions. However, pollution and human activities are damaging the environment. We must protect it for future generations.

Causes of Pollution

  • Industrial waste
  • Vehicle emissions
  • Plastic usage

Effects of Pollution

  1. Global warming
  2. Health problems
  3. Climate change
